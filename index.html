Where It's Cool To Be Irrational

This event was free and open to the community. We had over 500 visitors and more than 30 demonstrators. Showcased projects ranged from Raspberry Pi computing, Linux computing, 3D printing, UAV design, blacksmithing, soldering, kite making, electronics, robotics, pneumatics, virtual world technology, ham radio, and music.

  • What: A celebration for the DIY'er in all of us
  • When: Saturday, 14 March 2015
  • Where: WBI Tec^Edge Innovation and Collaboration Center, Dayton, OH
  • Why: To bring together various maker groups/businesses and encourage a collaborative DIY community in the Dayton area, to encourage exploration and tinkering as a path to innovation, to inspire future generations to become interested in STEM
  • Who: This event was free and open to the community. All ages welcome.
